Privacy Policy

Last updated: Monday, May 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Yell Records is a small, independently operated business based in Illinois. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you make a purchase, how we use that information, and the steps we take to protect it. By using our website or placing an order, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

  • First and last name
  • Shipping address, including street, city, state, and postal code
  • Email address and phone number
  • Order details, such as items purchased and transaction timestamps

We only collect the information necessary to process and fulfill your order. We do not collect passwords, payment card numbers, or any information unrelated to completing your purchase.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely for purposes related to processing and fulfilling your order. This includes preparing shipments, delivering digital purchases, providing receipts, preventing fraud, and maintaining accurate financial and tax records as required by law. We do not use your information for advertising, tracking, or any unrelated purposes.

4. How We Share Your Information

We only share your information with third parties when it is necessary to process your order. This includes payment processors such as PayPal, which handle all payment information on our behalf. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with advertisers or any unrelated third parties.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies on our website. We do not track user behavior, and we do not use third party analytics or advertising tools that place cookies in your browser.

6. Data Retention

We retain order information as long as necessary to comply with legal, tax, and accounting requirements. Order records cannot be deleted upon request. However, if we learn that personal information was provided by a child under 13, we will anonymize the personal information associated with the order while retaining the order record as required by law.

7. Your Rights

Because we only collect the information necessary to process and fulfill your order, there are no optional data uses to opt out of. However, you may contact us at any time to request access to the information associated with your order or to request that any personal information not required for legal, tax, or accounting purposes be anonymized. Order records themselves cannot be deleted, but personal information may be removed when permitted by law.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access. This includes using secure connections (HTTPS), limiting access to personal information, and relying on trusted third party providers for payment processing and data storage. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Yell Records is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will anonymize it. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will post the updated version on this page and update the “Last Updated” date at the top of the policy. Your continued use of the website after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
