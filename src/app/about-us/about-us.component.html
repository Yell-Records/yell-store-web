About Quantum Mart

A personal project developed by Matthew Cumbo, with help from Aaron Grau.

What Quantum Mart Is

Quantum Mart exists for the purpose of simulating a real-world e-commerce platform. You can purchase products, create product listings, use fictional currency, and walk through a typical shopping flow with no charge to any actual money.

Mission Statement

Make buying and selling simple, fast, and trustworthy for everyone.

Why We Built It

We wanted experience using the web-framework Angular while being able to build up something large enough for a flagship portfolio project.

Where This Is Going

There are no current plans for the website to ever go live, but who knows what will happen in the future. There are plans in place to containerize the application, but that won't be until the website is production-ready.

Take a look around! Make an account, create a product listing, and interact at your disposal!
